The Equity Effect — Socio-Economic Advantage vs NAPLAN Score Each bubble = one school · bubble size = student enrolment · ICSEA 1000 = national average socio-educational advantage
ICSEA explains ~82% of NAPLAN variance. A student at a high-advantage school (ICSEA 1150) is predicted to score ~55 points higher than one at a low-advantage school (ICSEA 850) — regardless of whether the school is public or private. The sector gap largely disappears once socio-economic advantage is controlled for.
